Academy

The FML Academy provides best practice resources and real-life applications pertaining to Web3, Metaverse, DeFi and DeSci. Acting as a connector to the global practitioners and industry leaders, the FML Academy is where passionate enthusiasts, experts and leaders come together to connect, share, learn and grow.

FML Academy programming is designed to tackle the key challenges that impact Web3 adoption. The practitioner-led intelligence is delivered via 2 types of digital sessions - Recorded and Live. Each speaker and a content creator is hand picked as having proved economically successful in their respective fields.

Recorded Sessions

The recorded sessions focus on 3 key areas:

  • 1.

    Most innovate ways crypto use cases are being implemented

  • 2.

    What is the innovative solution solving

  • 3.

    Team/technical skills required to implement innovative solutions

Live Sessions

Live sessions focus on the critical thinking process behind innovative solutions in Web3 and crypto use cases.

Public Town Square

An app for global digital communication focusing on 3 key areas. Information, Discourse, and Networking.

Information

Predictions - All verified content creators have proved economically successful in their respective fields.

Factual Information - Verified individuals/entities will post summarized white papers, case studies, and research in their respective fields. Upvote system that maintains quality

Discourse

Hot Discussions - Hottest/most engaged discussions in the last 72 hours.

Recommended Discussions - Feed is personalized based on areas of interest and is populated by predictions, factual information, and popular threads. Private DMs - Feed is personalized based on areas of interest and is populated by predictions, factual information, and popular threads.

Networking

5 Tiered Networking System - Individuals tier up by submitting a request once they meet the score requirements (weighting scale is determined by financial success and recognized accomplishments from reputable institutions.

Speed Networking - Elevator pitch styled networking rooms that can be filtered based on skill requirements. This is great for finding teams for projects.

Exclusive Networking - Access granted to winners of hackathons and whales. These special rooms open up periodically based on event schedules. Whales are accredited investors who have at least $1M in liquid assets.

IRL Events

The 3 areas of focus are special events, competitions, and quarterly conferences.

Special Events

Access granted to winners of hackathons and whales. These special rooms open up periodically based on event schedules. Whales are accredited investors who have at least $1M in liquid assets.

Competitions

Hackathons curated for innovation and networking in the fields of NFTs, Metaverse, and DeFi.

Quarterly Conferences

We go over our FML Transparency Report which covers our future plans, our growth, and projected growth. These conferences are also designed for low to mid level networking which will host keynote speakers as well.
